# Acme Corporation HR Wiki

Welcome to the Acme Corporation HR Wiki, your one-stop destination for all things related to employee benefits and information. Here at Acme Corporation, we believe in taking care of our employees like family, because after all, we're all in this together!

## Retirement Planning Options

### 401K Plan with Money, Money, Money, Inc.
At Acme Corporation, we offer a generous 401K plan through our trusted provider, Money, Money, Money, Inc. Employees can contribute to their retirement savings with a 100% match on the first 5% of their contributions. Planning for your future has never been easier or more rewarding!

### Life Insurance and Disability Coverage
We understand the importance of financial security, which is why we provide life insurance for all employees and offer optional disability insurance. Rest assured knowing that you and your loved ones are protected in case the unexpected happens.

### Spot Bonuses and Recognition
Hard work doesn't go unnoticed at Acme Corporation! We believe in recognizing and rewarding our employees for their dedication. That's why we offer $1000 spot bonuses for exceptional performance. Your efforts truly make a difference here.

### Fertility Benefits through Parenthood, Inc.
Family planning is a personal journey, and we support our employees every step of the way. Acme Corporation partners with Parenthood, Inc. to provide fertility benefits, ensuring that our employees have access to the resources they need to start or grow their families.

### Vacation, Holidays, and Sick Time
Work-life balance is essential, which is why we offer generous time off policies. Employees enjoy 25 days of vacation, 5 floating holidays, and 7 sick days per year. Take the time you need to recharge and come back refreshed and ready to tackle new challenges.

### Gym Memberships Reimbursement
We believe in promoting a healthy lifestyle, both physically and mentally. That's why we reimburse employees for gym memberships, encouraging everyone to prioritize their well-being. Stay active, stay healthy, and stay happy at Acme Corporation.

Leading the way at Acme Corporation is our CEO, Fredward Benson of Seattle, WA. With a background in digital media and a passion for innovation, Fredward brings a wealth of experience to our company. His leadership inspires us to reach new heights and achieve success together.
