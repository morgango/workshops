## Our Employee Benefits

At Acme Corporation, we value our employees and their well-being. That's why we offer a comprehensive benefits package that includes top-notch perks such as a 100% match on the first 5% of your 401K contributions through our trusted provider, Money, Money, Money, Inc. We also provide health insurance through Purple Shield of Yorba Linda, LLC, and offer optional disability insurance and life insurance for all employees.

## Recognition and Rewards

We believe in recognizing and rewarding hard work and dedication. That's why we offer $1000 spot bonuses for exceptional performance and achievements. At Acme, we understand the importance of acknowledging and appreciating our employees' contributions to the success of the company.

## Work-Life Balance

At Acme Corporation, we understand the importance of maintaining a healthy work-life balance. That's why we offer generous vacation time, including 25 days of vacation per year, 5 days of floating holidays, and 7 days of sick leave. We also reimburse for gym memberships to promote a healthy lifestyle among our employees.

## Fertility Benefits and Wellness Programs

We care about the well-being of our employees and their families. That's why we provide fertility benefits through Parenthood, Inc., to support those who are starting or expanding their families. Additionally, we offer wellness programs and meditation services through Headspace to help our employees achieve mental and physical well-being.
